   Various Tags used on HTML

  • Bold: strong
  • Underline: u
  • Size of head letters: h1~h6
  • New line: br
  • New Paragraph: p
  • Image: img, src, width
  • Circles for list: li
  • Tab: ul
  • Tab followed by number: ol
  • Title: title
  • Printing by same character style: utf-8
  • Assumbing about code: head
  • Main part: body
  • The most first Tag: !DOCTYPE html
  • Hypertext reference: a href
  • Opening hyperlink thru new tab: a href, target="_blank"
  • Explaing about a hyperlink when touched: a href, title=""
  • Blank: & and nbsp and ;
